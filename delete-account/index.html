Request Account & Data Deletion

MyHealthyGlucose is developed by DataSpark Tech LLC. If you'd like your account and all associated data (glucose logs, food logs, medication records, HbA1c entries, and daily goals) permanently deleted, send a request to:

Email: connect@datasparktech.com
Subject line: Account Deletion Request

Please include the email address associated with your Google Sign-In account so we can locate and delete the correct records. We will confirm and complete deletion within 30 days of a verified request, in accordance with our Privacy Policy.

Deleting your account removes your data from our cloud storage (Google Firestore). Uninstalling the app alone does not delete your cloud-stored data — you must submit a request as described above.
