Why We're Building This

The food deserved better.

Most diabetes apps were built around a Western pantry — cereal, sandwiches, pasta — and treat everything else as an edge case. If you grew up on dal, adobo, pho, or shawarma, you already know what that's like: logging your actual dinner and getting a shrug instead of a real number.

MyHealthyGlucose started with one region done properly — Indian regional cooking, real carb estimates, real cultural context — instead of a thousand cuisines done shallowly. It's since grown into a full diabetes companion with over 1,100 foods, blood pressure, medications, caregiver sharing and voice logging — still built around how you actually live, one real feature at a time.

— The MyHealthyGlucose team, DataSpark Tech LLC
